# Changelog

All relevant changes to the Libre methodology are documented in this file.

---

## v0.1.1 -- Justification pass

Addresses feedback on insufficient justification of principles, mechanisms, and assumptions.

- Each of the five principles now includes a "why" paragraph explaining its rationale
- Reconciled autonomy principle with team-level technical decisions (sovereignty applies within a task, collective decisions for what affects the team)
- Workflow states explained: why these six, why no "Ready" state, why Blocked branches off
- "One person, one task" justified with context switching, WIP limits, and accountability reasoning
- Milestones now include explicit comparison with sprints and what problem they solve
- New section: "When Libre works and when it does not", with explicit applicability conditions
- All changes applied to README.md, docs/index.html, and docs/es/index.html

---

## v0.1 -- Work in progress

First version of Libre. Defines the foundational elements of the methodology.

- Five principles: radical visibility, autonomy, mutual aid, voluntary agreement, direct action
- Workflow with six states: idea, confirmed, in progress, blocked, review, done
- Milestones with committed date and content
- Team agreements in two layers: non-negotiable (Layer 1) and team-decided (Layer 2)
- Team agreements template (`AGREEMENTS.md`)
- Comparison with Scrum
- Explicit removal of dailies, estimates, sprints, process roles, and recurring meetings
