## Reminder: the non-negotiables

These agreements come with Libre. They are not modified.

1. Everyone knows and shares the product goal
2. Work is visible to everyone at all times
3. No one assigns work to others, tasks are taken
4. Whoever takes a task carries it through to done
5. Tasks should be small but with their own purpose
6. External blockers are documented and actively tracked
